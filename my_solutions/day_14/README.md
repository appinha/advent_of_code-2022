# Day 14: Regolith Reservoir

## Summary

📃 **Input:** the scan of a two-dimensional vertical slice of the cave.

⭐ **Part One:** simulate the falling sand to find how many units of sand come to rest before sand starts flowing into the abyss below.

⭐ **Part Two:** simulate the falling sand until the source of the sand becomes blocked, then find how many units of sand come to rest.
